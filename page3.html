Cookies

You'll need:

  • 2 cups butter (460g), melted
  • 2 cups brown sugar (440g), packed
  • 2 cups granulated sugar (400g)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour (625g)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 cups chocolate chips (700g)

LET'S GET COOKIE-ING !!!!!

  • Preheat oven to 375 F.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the brown sugar, granulated sugar, add melted butter, until evenly combined and light in color.
  • Add in the eggs and vanilla, mixing until smooth.
  • Add the flour and baking power, folding the mixture until it forms a smooth dough.
  • Fold in the chocolate chips until evenly combined.
  • Using an ice cream scoop, scoop 6 balls of dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake for 12 mintues, then serve!
